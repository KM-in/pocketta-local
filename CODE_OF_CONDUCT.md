# Code of Conduct

PocketTA follows the Contributor Covenant, version 2.1. Contributors must help maintain a welcoming, harassment-free community regardless of background, identity, experience, or ability.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, constructive, and considerate.
- Accept responsibility, apologize for mistakes, and focus on what benefits the community.
- Protect private recordings, transcripts, credentials, and vulnerability details.

Harassment, discriminatory language, threats, unwanted sexual attention, deliberate intimidation, and publication of others' private information are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately through the repository host's maintainer contact or security-reporting channel. Maintainers will review reports promptly, protect reporter privacy where possible, and may warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently remove participants based on impact and recurrence.

This policy is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct.html).
